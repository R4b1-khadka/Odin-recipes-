Boiled egg

boiling an egg is fairly easy task. there are only few things that you need to do.
why even boil egg first? because it's high in protein and calories and easy to make.
In this post there's everthing you need to know about boiling an egg.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water
  • salt
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Take a pot and fill it with water
  2. Take your egg and put it in a pot
  3. Add salt and vinegar. This helps to peal off the outer cover easily
  4. Boil for 10 to 15 minutes
  5. Take out the egg after 15 minutes and put it in cold water. Peal off the outer cover and enjoy your boiled egg
